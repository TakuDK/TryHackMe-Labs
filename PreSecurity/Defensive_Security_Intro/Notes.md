## What is Defensive Security
Defensive security focuses on protecting systems, detecting attacks, and responding to incidents.

The people who do this work are called the Blue Team.

## SOC
A Security Operations Center (SOC) monitors networks, servers and devices for suspicious activity.

## DFIR
DFIR stands for Digital Forensics and Incident Response.
It deals with investigating cyber attacks and recovering systems.

## SIEM
A SIEM collects logs and alerts so analysts can see threats in real time.
